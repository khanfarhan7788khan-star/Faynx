Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a beautiful digital environment. Faynx was built to be the cleanest, fastest, and most honest free wallpaper resource on the web — no ads cluttering the experience, no watermarks on downloads, no account required.

We curate our collection across six carefully chosen categories: Nature, Anime, Cars, Space, Minimal, and Aesthetic — covering the most-searched wallpaper styles globally.

Image Quality & Sources

Every image on Faynx is sourced from Unsplash, one of the world's largest repositories of freely-licensed photography. Images are contributed by talented photographers worldwide and available under the Unsplash License, which permits free use for personal and commercial purposes.

Every wallpaper is served in multiple resolutions — Full HD (1920×1080), 2K (2560×1440), 4K UHD (3840×2160), and mobile-optimised portrait formats for iPhone and Android — delivered in WebP format for the fastest possible load times with no compromise on quality.
