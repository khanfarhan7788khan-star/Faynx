Faynx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit faynx.tech, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and referring URL — collected via anonymised analytics.
  • Download events: We record when wallpaper downloads occur (without linking them to individual users) to understand popular content.
  • Account data (optional): If you create an account via Firebase Authentication, we store your email address and display name. Google Sign-In is processed by Google and subject to their privacy policy.
  • Contact form submissions: Name and email address when you voluntarily contact us.

We do not collect payment information, sell advertising, or track you across other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the wallpaper browsing and download service
  • To save your preferences (theme, layout, saved wallpapers) — stored in your browser or Firebase
  • To understand which content is most popular via aggregated analytics
  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To maintain and improve the website

3. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics in anonymised/IP-anonymised mode, where applicable)
  • Remembering your theme and layout preferences
  • PWA service worker caching for offline support
  • Firebase Authentication session tokens (if you create an account)

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or third-party tracking. You can clear cookies at any time in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Unsplash: Images are served from Unsplash's CDN. When images load, your browser connects to Unsplash servers. See Unsplash's privacy policy.

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore for optional account features. Data is processed by Google. See Firebase's privacy policy.

Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts. See Google's privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Anonymised analytics data is retained for up to 26 months. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. Account data (email, display name, saved wallpapers) is retained until you delete your account. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@faynx.tech. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the data we hold. Account authentication is handled by Firebase, which uses industry-standard security practices. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of Faynx after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@faynx.tech or via our contact page.
