Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-19

Faynx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit faynx.tech, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, and referring URL via anonymised analytics.
  • Download events: We record when wallpaper downloads occur (without linking them to individual users).
  • Contact form submissions: Name and email address when you contact us voluntarily.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics (e.g. Google Analytics in anonymised mode)
  • Remembering your theme/layout preferences
  • PWA service worker functionality for offline support

We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

Images on Faynx are served via Unsplash. When images load, your browser may connect to Unsplash's servers. Please review Unsplash's privacy policy for details on how they handle requests.

We may use Google Analytics for aggregated, anonymised traffic analysis. IP anonymisation is enabled.

4. Data Retention

We retain anonymised analytics data for up to 26 months. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@faynx.tech.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@faynx.tech or visit our contact page.
